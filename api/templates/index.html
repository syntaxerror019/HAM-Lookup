Instructions

To use the HAM Radio Callsign Lookup site, follow these steps:

  1. Enter the callsign you want to look up in the "Callsign" field.
  2. Click the "Lookup" button to submit the form.
  3. The license information for the entered callsign will be displayed below.

Information

This website utilizes the www.wt9v.net website to perform HAM radio callsign lookups. When a callsign is entered and the form is submitted, our backend service sends a request to www.wt9v.net to retrieve the relevant license information. The website generates a PDF containing the license details, which our service then scrapes to extract the necessary information. This data is subsequently displayed on our site for your convenience.

It is important to note that the data provided on this site is not owned by us. The information is publicly available and sourced from www.wt9v.net. While we strive to ensure the accuracy and reliability of the data presented, we cannot guarantee that it is 100% accurate or up-to-date. Users should verify the information independently if it is critical for their purposes. We disclaim any liability for errors or omissions in the data provided.
